Welcome to Chasing Embers

Chasing Embers is a two-piece mostly acoustic duo, playing a mix of modern pop and mellow rock. Stephanie leads with her expressive vocals, while Jason provides piano and guitar, along with a few backing tracks created in his home studio. Together, we aim to create enjoyable, memorable performances for both public venues and private events.
